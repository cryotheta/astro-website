EVENTS

Workshop cum Public lecture on
"The Nature of the Universe and its Origin"

Event conducted by Devashish Kaushik (Speaker), Saurabh Kumar and Shiv Kumar

Previous Next


  • Date: 10th August 2019
  • Venue: 1B 201
  • Time: 3pm to 5pm



Highlights -


  • Topics relevant such as History of the Universe, Theory of gravity, Rotation Curve & Dark matter, Big Bang, Dark Energy, etc. were introduced to the audience.
  • Event started with Introduction to Astro Club and its activities by Club Head, Saurabh Kumar
  • Event consisted of talk and Question and Answer session by Devashish Kaushik and serving of refreshments to audience
  • Video shown on Big Bang
  • Detailed feedback collection from First Year participants
  • Attendees included B. Tech. students from all years and branches
